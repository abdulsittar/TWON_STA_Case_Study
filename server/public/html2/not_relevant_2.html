Serbia to Launch National Esports Championship in 2027
Globalne novosti

Serbia is preparing to host its first-ever National Esports Championship in 2027, reflecting the rapid growth of competitive gaming in the country. The event will feature tournaments in popular games such as Counter-Strike, League of Legends, and Dota 2, attracting both local and international players.

Esports has seen a dramatic rise in popularity in Serbia over the past decade, with many young gamers achieving international success. The championship aims to provide a platform for up-and-coming talent, helping to establish Serbia as a key player in the global esports scene.

Government and private sector investments in esports infrastructure have increased in recent years, leading to the creation of state-of-the-art gaming arenas and training centers. Organizers of the championship are working closely with gaming companies and sponsors to ensure the event meets international standards.

The championship is expected to bring economic benefits as well, with gaming tourism on the rise. Fans from neighboring countries may travel to Serbia to attend the event, boosting local businesses and further promoting the country's gaming industry. With esports now recognized as a legitimate profession, this initiative could open new career paths for Serbian players, coaches, and content creators.
