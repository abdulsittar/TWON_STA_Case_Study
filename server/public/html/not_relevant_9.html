EXIT Festival 2025: Headliners Announced, Including Major International Acts

Vesti iz sveta

Serbia’s world-famous EXIT Festival has revealed its first wave of headliners for the 2025 edition, and the lineup is already generating massive buzz. Leading the bill are British rock giants The Killers, along with electronic music superstar Martin Garrix and regional favorite Senidah.

The festival, held at the Petrovaradin Fortress from July 10-13, will also feature performances by Stormzy, Amelie Lens, and Serbian hip-hop sensation Gru. Early bird tickets sold out in record time, with organizers expecting over 200,000 attendees.

"EXIT continues to prove itself as one of Europe’s top music festivals," said founder Dušan Kovačević. "This year, we’re blending global stars with the best of Balkan talent."

Fans are particularly excited about The Killers’ return to Serbia after their 2019 show, which drew one of the largest crowds in EXIT history. Meanwhile, Senidah’s performance is set to be a highlight, following her recent chart-topping hits.

With more acts to be announced, EXIT 2025 is shaping up to be an unforgettable celebration of music and culture.
